Quick Take

Best for building with APIs: Deliverect — developer-friendly, allows custom channels to plug in and push clean orders directly to Toast and other POS systems.

Best North America ops: ItsaCheckmate — practical for multi-location brands with Toast; operator-friendly console and simple tiered pricing.

Best single-vendor stack: Chowly — delivery→POS + first-party ordering + marketing/loyalty in one place.

How they differ

Chowly vs Deliverect: Chowly is the “one-stop shop” — it doesn’t just connect delivery apps to POS, it also gives you first-party ordering and marketing tools in the same box. Deliverect leans “builder-friendly”: it centralizes menus and gives developers more freedom to plug in custom channels (like a voice agent) and scale across many countries.

Chowly vs ItsaCheckmate: Both solve tablet chaos and push orders into POS, but Chowly adds more marketing/loyalty features, while ItsaCheckmate focuses on an easy operational console for North American brands (very Toast-friendly) with simple tiered pricing.

Deliverect vs ItsaCheckmate: Deliverect is the global/API-first choice for chains that want to build on top of the platform and manage huge menu footprints. ItsaCheckmate is the pragmatic choice for US/Canada operators that want reliable marketplace + POS syncing with minimal fuss.
